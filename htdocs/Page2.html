The Swinging Sixties (1960s)


The Swinging Sixties was a decade of change: a change in people’s values (brought by the Civil Rights movement and rallies) and consequently, a change in
the consumable media. As a result, controversial, but well-loved, movies like “Breathless”, “Psycho”, and “Belle de Jour” were released during this time. However,
watching movies can still only be done in one way: scheduling a trip to the cinema, going to the cinema, and getting yourself a ticket. This has been the only method
of watching movies for 4 decades now.
