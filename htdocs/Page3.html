The Me Decade (1970s)


The Me Decade ushered in possibly one of the most important inventions to film: the invention of the VHS. The VHS was completely revolutionary at the time,
as staying at home to watch a movie was unheard of. This new method of consuming media taught people that they can actually watch films from the comfort of their home.
Ultimately, it foreshadowed the need now for quick and easy access to content.
