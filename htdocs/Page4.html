The Early Aughts (2000s)


The Internet was still a fairly new thing in the Early Aughts, and this period was a rapid change for many. The Iphone was invented, new technology was available, and the world was in the midst of a Technological Revolution. However, even with all this, going to the cinema to watch a movie was still the norm. Even DVD Rental Shops like Video City were in full force. Overall, ways to consume media and film was spread out: there were many ways to watch films, unlike say 30 years before.
